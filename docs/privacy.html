Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Your privacy is important to us.

SnipZip was designed to work on your Mac, not in the cloud. We don't ask you to create an account, and we don't upload the files you archive or extract.

Your files stay on your Mac

When you browse, create, or extract an archive, the work happens locally on your device.

We do not collect, upload, or store the contents of your files or archives.

We don't collect personal data from the app

SnipZip doesn't require an account and doesn't collect personal information through normal use of the app.

Purchasing SnipZip Pro

If you purchase SnipZip Pro, your payment is handled entirely by Apple through the Mac App Store.

We never see or store your payment information.

If you contact us

If you send us an email for support or feedback, we'll receive the information you choose to include, such as your email address and the contents of your message.

We'll only use that information to respond to you.

Crash reports

If you've chosen to share crash reports or diagnostic information with Apple, Apple may send us reports that help identify and fix bugs.

These reports are provided through Apple's systems and are subject to Apple's privacy practices.

Our website

The SnipZip website may collect standard technical information, such as server logs, to keep the site secure and running smoothly.

Changes

If we make significant changes to how SnipZip handles information, we'll update this page and revise the date at the top.

Questions?

If you have any questions about privacy, feel free to contact us:

support@snipzip.app
